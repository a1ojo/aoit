Next-generation network (NGN) is a generic term used to describe emerging packet-based networks that will transform the 21st century landscape. Such networks feature mixed traffic types such as voice, video, and data, and each traffic type receives an appropriate class of service. Realizing such converged networks requires special-purpose technology in the network, such as MPLS.
